Emotional Intelligence

1. What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence (EQ) or Emotional Quotient is the awareness of one's emotions and of others'. This awareness forms the basis of informed personal decisions. This eliminates impulsive and reactive responses.

2. How is EQ different to IQ?

If EQ is emotional awareness, Intellegence Quotient (IQ) is the abililty to use reasoning and logic, problem solving and translate complex ideas.

3. Why is EQ important?

I think EQ is important for two main reasons:

  1. We are surrounded by stress. It may be at home, at work or at school but it is almost always present in varying degree. However, how we react or respond to it is up to us. This is where are EQ comes in. The higher EQ we have, the more stable we are when these stressful situations come up.
  2. As they say, no man is an island. We are always dealing with people. How we interact and communicate with them is a sign of our EQ maturity.
